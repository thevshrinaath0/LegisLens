RENTAL AGREEMENT (Medium Risk Draft)

This Rental Agreement is made on this 1st day of March 2024.

LANDLORD: TechSpace Ventures Ltd.
TENANT: Alpha Startups Inc.

1. TERM AND TERMINATION
The lease is for 3 years. The Landlord may terminate this agreement with 30 days' notice for any reason. The Tenant must provide 90 days' notice and pay a termination fee equal to 1 month's rent to terminate early.

2. RENT
Monthly rent is INR 75,000. The Landlord reserves the right to review and increase the rent based on market rates annually, with a minimum increase of 10%.

3. INDEMNITY
The Tenant agrees to indemnify the Landlord against all claims, losses, and liabilities arising from the Tenant's use of the premises. This indemnity covers all legal fees and is not limited by the security deposit.

4. MAINTENANCE
The Tenant is responsible for all day-to-day maintenance and minor repairs. Structural repairs will be shared 50:50 between Landlord and Tenant.

5. PENALTY
Late rent payments will attract a penalty charge of INR 1,000 per day until payment is made.

6. JURISDICTION
All disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Delhi. (Note: Tenant is based in Bangalore).

7. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to keep the terms of this agreement confidential.

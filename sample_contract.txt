RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is made on this 1st day of February 2024, by and between:

LANDLORD: MegaCorp Holdings Pvt Ltd.
TENANT: Small Business Solutions LLP.

1. TERM
The lease shall be for a fixed period of 5 years. Both parties have the right to terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

2. RENT
The monthly rent shall be INR 50,000. Rent increases shall be mutually agreed upon annually, capped at 5%.

3. INDEMNITY
The Tenant agrees to indemnify the Landlord against direct damages caused by the Tenant's negligence. The Landlord agrees to indemnify the Tenant against structural defects. Liability is capped at 3 months' rent.

4. MAINTENANCE
Minor repairs (< INR 5000) shall be the responsibility of the Tenant. Major structural repairs shall be the responsibility of the Landlord.

5. PENALTY
If the rent is delayed by more than 7 days, a simple interest of 1% per month will be charged on the outstanding amount.

6. JURISDICTION
Any disputes arising from this agreement shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts in [City of Property], India.

7. USE OF PREMISES
The Tenant may make non-structural alterations to the property with prior written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.
